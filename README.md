## What it does

- **Pick your photos** — add folders and/or individual photos; filter by **date range**,
  **location/city**, **favorites**, or a **quality score** (sharpness/contrast/exposure).
- **Two ways to play:**
  - **Static** — render an upload-ready **MP4** (1080p or 4K, H.264) for YouTube or sharing.
  - **Dynamic** — a private **live viewer** in your browser that shuffles your photos forever;
    bookmark its address on a phone, tablet, or TV as an always-on digital photo frame.
- **Music** — add your own audio (looped to a clean ending), or open a web music service
  in the live viewer.
- **Transitions & motion** — pick which effects to use (crossfade, wipe, slide, zoom, iris,
  Ken Burns, and more), applied at random; slow Ken Burns pan/zoom on each photo.
- **Automatic captions** — each photo is captioned with its **location and date/time**
  (editable), derived from the photo's own metadata — offline, no internet needed.
- **Collages & vignettes**, per-photo rotate, "don't use" marks, and batch rendering of
  multiple projects.

Full guides are built into the app under **Help → Quick Start / User Guide**.
